Transcript Request

Request your official USIU–Africa transcript for academic, professional, or personal use. Transcripts can be reviewed online before submitting your request and are available for download in official PDF format.

How to Request Your Transcript

  1. Log in to your USIU–Africa Student Portal.
  2. Navigate to Academics > Transcripts.
  3. Review your unofficial transcript for accuracy.
  4. Click Request Official Transcript and fill out the required form.
  5. Pay the transcript processing fee via the Fee Payment section.
  6. Once processed, you will receive an email with a secure download link.
